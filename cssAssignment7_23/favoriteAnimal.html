Fiordland penguin, (Eudyptes pachyrhynchus), also called Fiordland crested penguin, Fiordland also spelled Fjordland, species of crested penguin (genus Eudyptes, order Sphenisciformes) characterized by a thick stripe of pale yellow feather plumes above each eye (the superciliary stripe) that extends from the bill to the rear of the head
